About Diagnostics

Welcome to S.B Diagnostics, your trusted partner in healthcare and diagnostic services in Sinzai, More, Kachcha Katra Rd, Uttar Pradesh. Established over a decade ago, we have been committed to providing accurate and reliable diagnostic services to our community. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced technology to ensure the highest standards of care and precision.
